Request Account & Data Deletion

Effective Date: June 20, 2024

We are committed to protecting your privacy. If you would like to request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Submit a Data Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your account and data, please send an email to contact@froots.co with the following information:

Email Subject: Data Deletion Request for [your phone number]

Email Body:

Please include the following details in your email:

  • Registered Phone Number:To help us identify your account
  • Contact Number In case your registered number is different from the one you use to communicate, please share the number so our team can get in touch with you and verify your identity.

You can use the following link to send the email Send mail now

What Happens Next

Once we receive your email, we will:

  • Confirm Receipt: We will acknowledge your request within 7 days and may reach out for further verification if necessary.
  • Process the Request: Your request will be processed within 90 days. All your account data, including personal information, will be permanently deleted from our systems.
  • Final Confirmation: You will receive a final confirmation once the deletion process is complete.

Important Notice

  • Permanent Deletion: By requesting the deletion of your account, you understand that all associated data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
  • Legal Compliance: Certain data may be retained for legal, security, or operational reasons, in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to contact us at contact@froots.co.
